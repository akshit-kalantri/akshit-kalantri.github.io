Adventures of Jason Turing

A two part fiction series- adventure novels revolving around the life of college student Jason Turing, who finds himself thrust into a life-altering and deadly plot.

Unlikeliest of Heroes

Have you ever felt that you were just another person in a faceless crowd? Awkward college student Jason Turing felt this way, until his life changed, forever, in a day. Join him on his journey as he discovers aspects of his character that were hidden even from him. His path was not chosen by him, but instead chosen for him. A path that will put everything he knows and everyone he trusts to the ultimate test. He is, most certainly, the unlikeliest of heroes.
